Practice good hand hygiene. Wash your hands often with soap and water or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer.

Keep a safe distance from people who sneeze or cough.

Wear a mask when surrounded by other people.

Do not touch your eyes, mouth, or nose with your hands.

Cover your mouth and nose with an elbow fold or handkerchief when coughing or sneezing.

If you feel unwell, stay at home.
